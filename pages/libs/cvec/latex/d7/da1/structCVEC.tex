\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Custom generic dynamic vector type. 

\doxylink{structCVEC}{CVEC} is a flexible container that stores a sequence of elements of uniform size. It behaves similarly to std\+::vector in C++, supporting dynamic resizing, insertion, deletion, merging, reversing, and splitting.
